I'll Meet you There!

Installations
Bought a TV or a surround sound system? We can install it for you! Two of the most frustrating parts about buying a new device is setting it up and learning how it works. Denny's Radio & TV takes the hassle out of installing your new device. Once installed Denny will give you a basic tutorial on how it works.
On-Site Repairs
If you have a device that you are unable to transport to Denny's Radio & TV call the shop and let us know. If the issue can't be troubleshot I will come to you. On-site repairs are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
Antenna Service and Repair
If you have a bad signal, damaged antenna, or other issue give us a call. We have a wide variety of tools and experience to diagnose your antenna system and get you ready for the next big game!
Troubleshooting
Whether by phone or in person, Denny's Radio & TV will assist you through confusing manuals, disconnected cords, and menus that just won't go away. A friendly attitude accompanied by a vast knowledge of device functions is used for every troubleshooting session.
