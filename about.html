History

Denny's Radio & TV was founded in 1974 by Denny Klick. Denny's philosophy was providing great service to the people of Thorp, Wisconsin and the surrounding area who required electronics repair and sales. When Denny opened his business he started with one antenna, a tube caddy, and a single distributer of electronic devices. Throughout his 41 years in business, Denny's knowledge and resources changed but his philosophy has stayed the same. Now, 41 years later, Denny has a great inventory of electronic items for purchase, parts for easy repairs, and multiple distribution resources to order from. Experience

As the proprietor, Denny Klick has over 41 years of experience in the electronics field. Besides his formal training in electronics repair, Denny has continued his training to keep current with technology giving him the ability to have a wide array of knowledge about all aspects of electronic repair. In addition to his electronics knowledge, Denny strives to make his customer experience positive no matter the repair, service, or sale. No matter the level of knowledge his guests have, Denny ensures they walk away happy and informed.
